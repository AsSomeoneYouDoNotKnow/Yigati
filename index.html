About Yigati Chemicals LLP

Established in November 2020, Yigati Chemicals LLP is a leading chemical waste management plant. We specialize in recycling industrial waste and provide high-quality bromine for pharmaceutical industries. Our mission is to contribute to a cleaner, more sustainable future by offering eco-friendly solutions for chemical waste disposal.

Our operations are based in Aurangabad, Maharashtra, and we are fully committed to adhering to the highest safety and environmental standards in all our processes.

Our Services

  • Comprehensive recycling of industrial chemical waste.
  • Recovery and purification of bromine for pharmaceutical-grade applications.
  • Commitment to sustainability and reducing industrial ecological footprints.
  • Compliance with all relevant environmental regulations.
