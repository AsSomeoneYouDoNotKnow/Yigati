About Yigati Chemicals LLP

Established in November 2020, Yigati Chemicals LLP is a Limited Liability Partnership firm registered in Mumbai, Maharashtra. We specialize in recycling industrial chemical waste, focusing on the recovery and purification of bromine for pharmaceutical applications.

Our commitment to sustainability and adherence to international environmental regulations ensure that we provide eco-friendly solutions to minimize industrial ecological footprints.

Our Services

  • Comprehensive recycling of industrial chemical waste.
  • Recovery and purification of bromine for pharmaceutical-grade applications.
  • Consultation on sustainable waste management practices.
  • Compliance assistance with environmental regulations.
