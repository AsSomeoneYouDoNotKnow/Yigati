Company Overview

Yigati Chemicals LLP is a leading chemical waste management company based in Aurangabad, Maharashtra. Founded in November 2020, we specialize in recycling industrial waste, transforming it into valuable by-products like bromine for the pharmaceutical industry.

Our operations are guided by the principles of sustainability and environmental stewardship, ensuring that we meet all safety and environmental regulations while contributing to a cleaner, greener future.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to provide effective and eco-friendly solutions for industrial chemical waste disposal, while our vision is to be a leader in promoting circular economy practices in the chemical industry.

Leadership

The company is spearheaded by two experienced professionals, Shyamsundar Vasantrao Ughan and Devendra Dinkarrao Deshmukh, who bring a wealth of industry knowledge and commitment to innovation and sustainability.
